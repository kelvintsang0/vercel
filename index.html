What we do

Three ways we
deliver value

Scoped, time-boxed engagements built to leave your team stronger, not dependent on us indefinitely.

01 — Assessment
Govern Ready Audit
A structured 4–6 week diagnostic of your governance posture, covering metadata, data quality, lineage, compliance, and AI readiness, culminating in a scored report and prioritized remediation roadmap.
  • Governance maturity scorecard (DAMA-DMBOK2)
  • AI readiness rating with specific risk flags
  • Prioritized 90-day remediation roadmap
  • Executive presentation deck
Fixed scope · $8,000 – $15,000 CAD
02 — Retainer
Data Steward Retainer
Embedded governance leadership on a monthly basis. We act as your fractional data governance function, running stewardship programs, onboarding tooling, and building operating models that stick.
  • Weekly governance working sessions
  • Metadata catalog setup & ongoing stewardship
  • Data quality monitoring & alerting
  • Policy authoring and SOP development
Ongoing · from $8,000 / month CAD
03 — Delivery
Data Platform Build
End-to-end delivery of a modern lakehouse platform with governance embedded from the start, on Databricks or Microsoft Fabric, with dbt, Soda, and your reporting layer of choice.
  • Lakehouse architecture (Bronze / Silver / Gold)
  • ETL/ELT pipelines with dbt transformation layer
  • Embedded data quality controls with Soda
  • Power BI or Qlik reporting layer
Project-based · scoped on engagement
04 — Training
Stewardship Workshop
A one or two-day hands-on workshop that builds real governance capability in your team, calibrated to your tools and data environment, not a classroom lecture on theory.
  • DAMA-DMBOK2 applied to your specific context
  • Hands-on sessions with Atlan and Soda
  • Stewardship roles, RACI, and escalation design
  • Take-home playbook and policy templates
Workshop · $4,000 – $8,000 CAD / day
